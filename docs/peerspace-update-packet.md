# VibeShack Peerspace Update Packet

Use this packet to keep every Peerspace listing in line with the current VibeShack website: premium, specific, search-friendly, and clear about what is included.

The live title strategy follows Peerspace's own guidance: lead with the room type, the strongest use case, and the most concrete differentiator. Do not stuff "San Francisco" into every title because Peerspace already shows location in search results.

## Shared Rules Copy

Please return the studio to the condition it was in at arrival. Bag any trash and leave it inside the studio; our team will handle final disposal, equipment breakdown, and room reset. No glitter, confetti, smoke, haze, open flames, wall mounting, or paint without written approval. Food and drinks are allowed in approved areas, but please keep liquids away from cameras, microphones, lighting, and backdrops.

## Shared Safety Copy

Our team resets the studio between bookings, sanitizes high-touch surfaces, and checks cameras, microphones, lighting, and backdrop areas before each session. Guests are expected to keep walkways clear, follow crew instructions around lighting and stands, and report spills or equipment issues immediately.
