## Platform Strategy

Giggster listings should lead with the room type and buyer use case. Do not make titles cute or vague. Buyers are searching for production locations, so titles should say what the room is, what it is for, and the strongest concrete differentiator.

Brand architecture:

- VibeShack: media company and brand studio.
- VibeShack Studios: San Francisco production arm at 950 Battery St.
- Giggster listings: individual rentable production spaces inside VibeShack Studios.

No listing should use phone numbers, external URLs, or direct contact info because Giggster asks hosts not to add commercial names, addresses, contact info, or links inside listing copy.

## Rules Copy

Please return the studio to the condition it was in at arrival. Bag any trash and leave it inside the studio; our team will handle final disposal, equipment breakdown, and room reset. - Does the title match what the photo shows?
- Does the price match the VibeShack website?
- Does the description tell clients what they can make here?
- Are weak BTS photos pushed below strong room photos?
- Is calendar sync connected before instant booking is enabled?
