# VibeShack Google Business Profile Playbook

This is the weekly operating system for Google Business Profile. The goal is to make the profile behave like a second website for VibeShack Studios, while keeping the brand architecture clear: VibeShack is the media company and brand studio; VibeShack Studios is the San Francisco production arm.

Automation: `vibeshack-google-business-profile-upkeep`
Cadence: every Monday at 9 AM Pacific.

## Weekly Checklist

1. Add 3-5 new photos.
   - Use only high-quality approved studio photos.
   - Prioritize real rooms, real production setups, client-useful angles, and polished finished sets.
   - Avoid blurry BTS, screenshots, dark monitor closeups, duplicate angles, wires/clutter, and photos that misrepresent the room.

2. Publish one weekly post.
   - CTA should usually be Book.
   - Link to `https://www.vibeshackstudios.com/book/`.
   - Rotate topics: podcast studio, green screen, photo services, video production, white cyc, content days, tours.

3. Add or refresh one service/product card.
   - Keep service names aligned with the website.
   - Use real pricing only.
   - Use clean photos that match the service.

4. Ask recent happy clients for reviews.
   - Do not create fake reviews.
   - Do not pressure clients.
   - Ask for specific honest details: room used, type of shoot, what worked.

5. Add or answer one useful Q&A item.
   - Use questions real clients ask before booking.
   - Keep answers short, factual, and booking-focused.

6. Verify the booking link.
   - Primary booking URL: `https://www.vibeshackstudios.com/book/`
   - Tour URL: `https://www.vibeshackstudios.com/tour/`
   - Website URL: `https://www.vibeshackstudios.com/`

7. Check categories.
   - Company-level language can include media company, brand studio, or branding agency.
   - Location-level language should emphasize production studio intent.
   - Preferred physical-location categories where available: recording studio, video production service, photography studio, film production company, production studio.

## Monthly Checks

- Confirm hours still show open 24/7.
- Confirm address is exactly `950 Battery St, San Francisco, CA 94111`.
- Confirm photos still look premium and current.
- Confirm services and prices match the website.
- Confirm no third-party appointment provider has replaced the website booking link.
- Review competitor categories for "podcast studio San Francisco", "production studio San Francisco", and "green screen studio San Francisco".

## Photo Standards

Use photos that make the buyer understand the room in under two seconds:

- Wide room image first.
- Finished production setup second.
- Talent or use-case example third.
- Detail/mic/camera/lighting image fourth only if it is premium.
- No low-res screenshots.
- No repeated image angles.
- No harsh crops where the buyer cannot understand the room.
- No visible clutter, loose wires, monitor-only shots, or accidental behind-the-scenes images unless they look intentionally editorial.
